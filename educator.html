Philosophy
Focus

As a teacher, I am to assist students in develop critical thinking skills to develop a process for any problem.

  • Computer Science and Mathematics are difficult because it requiries a different method of thinking that is not explicitly developed in children.
  • By abstractly introducing this process, it allows for connections to personal experiences to be made further strengthening this process.
  • This model of logical problem solving is then ingrained as a tool for the students to use when navigating through any sort of problem.
Classroom Management

As a new teacher, I have been told by many experienced educators that classroom management is a major point of learning. My time as a student teacher was with a class that had an existing teacher who worked for many years to foster a culture within their class and school.
